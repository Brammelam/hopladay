Payment information

How we handle your payment securely

Payment processing

Payments are processed by Stripe. Your payment information is handled securely by Stripe and is not stored on our servers.

PCI DSS compliant
Stripe is certified to the highest level of PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard).
Encrypted connection
All data is transmitted over an encrypted SSL/TLS connection.

Payment methods

We support several payment methods through Stripe. Available options may vary by country and device.

Credit and debit cards
Major card networks such as Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.
Digital wallets
Where supported, wallets like Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Link by Stripe.
Local methods
In some regions, local payment methods such as bank transfers may be available.

Pricing

The price shown when you click “Upgrade” is the exact amount that will be charged. Any applicable taxes are included or clearly displayed before you pay. There are no hidden fees.
